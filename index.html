Slime Game Playtesting Report

Conducted and analyzed a comprehensive user research study of Slime Game as part of a semester-long project. Designed and ran 15 structured playtesting sessions, collecting both observational and interview data to evaluate player behavior, frustration points, and satisfaction. Applied mixed-methods analysis, combining descriptive statistics with qualitative coding, to identify patterns in difficulty curves, confusing mechanics, and player engagement. Chop Chop

Created a team-oriented physical board game, refining mechanics through implementation, critique, and playtesting. Designed engaging player interactions.
Game Description: Draw food, race the clock, and join forces with your fellow chefs to create masterpieces on the blank canvas of an empty plate. Chop Chop is a fast-paced, cooperative, drawing-based cooking game that’ll leave you hungry for more. You and one to four other gourmet chefs have been hired to cook up some dishes for impatient, hungry customers while navigating a chaotic, stressful kitchen. Chefs must finish all dishes within the time limit to make it to the next shift and keep their jobs. Better be quick on the draw! Chop chop!
